Josefa Llanes Escoda stands as a towering figure in Philippine history, known for her unwavering commitment to social reform, women's rights, and youth development. Her most enduring legacy is the founding of the Girl Scouts of the Philippines, an organization that has shaped the lives of countless young women across the nation.
